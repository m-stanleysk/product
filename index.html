About Happy Bee

Happy Bee is a little cartoon character I came up with for an assignment in my design class. A clumsy stroke of my stylus gave the smiling bee a determined little "eyebrow" that has remained a key feature of Happy Bee's facial expression as I have refined his design.

Happy Bee is the flagship character for my Print on Demand product shop, and has become my go-to icon on social platforms.

If you want a Happy Bee of your own on a t-shirt, sticker, magnet, or other apparel or household item (or just want to stay in the loop!) join our mailing list by submitting your e-mail address through the form above: Mailing List.
